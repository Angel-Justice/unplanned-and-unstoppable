From Chaos to Strategy

When I stumbled into the world of survivor empowerment in 2019, little did I know how my life would change. Armed with nothing but curiosity and maybe a little chaos, I didn't fully understand what I was getting into. What started as spontaneous "why not?" turned into a journey that changed everything. I found my tribe, we had purpose and power to make the change we wanted to see. From parliament to the people, we continue to engage in a thoughtful way, and with purpose to win hearts and minds. Our movement is built with compassion and kindness at the forefront. We share the stories behind the slogans and every small win shapes our next move. So, what next? A new adventure awaits.

Locks at Both Ends

They say justice is blind, but I know it can see;
It just turns its back when it's someone like me.
No papers in order, no lawyers to spare,
Just forms lost in systems that pretend to care.

I wait in a limbo you built, brick by brick,
Delays by design, each moment makes me sick.
From shouting for fairness in rooms built for 'No.'
Profits from my pain; this system is cold.

How much more must I endure?
I'm stuck in a queue with locks at both ends.
If healing is slow and justice unclear,
Then let this be known: I am still here.
a poem written by me
